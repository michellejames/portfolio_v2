Hi, I'm Shelly!

    In short - no reference to my height:
  • Oh so German
  • Crazy Cat Lady
  • Gourmet Cheese Lover
  • Soccer Fanatic
  • Passionate Traveler

I’m an Atlanta based web developer. Born and raised in Nuremberg, Germany, I decided to be adventurous and move to the U.S. in 2014. Because, that’s what you do - right?!

Back in Germany, I’ve majored in Marketing Communications and worked several years for one of Germany’s largest Public Relations agencies. I’ve worked with clients from many different industries - fashion, food and celebrities, just to name a few. Brining a brand “to life” by creating content that does not just inform but also move a customer on various levels was the reason I initially fell in love with marketing. But at some point, creating content and handing it off to someone else to built it wasn’t enough anymore - I wanted to be the creator!

After living in the U.S. for a year and pondering if going back to school is worth it I decided to be yet again adventurous and study Interactive Development - just to graduate 1.5 years later from The Creative Circus.

My passion to create has only become stronger during my time at The Creative Circus. I’m hungry for challenges, ready to solve them and deliver a product that will yet again make you move!
